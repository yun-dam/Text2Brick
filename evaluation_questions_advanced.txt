BRICK Data Advanced Evaluation Questions - FCU Sensors Dataset
================================================================

Complex Questions with Aggregations, Conditions, and Comparisons:
-----------------------------------------------------------------

1. Show me when the FCU was in cooling mode in the last 20 readings.

2. Find periods when room temperature exceeded the cooling setpoint.

3. What is the average cooling valve position when the FCU is operating in December?

4. Calculate the temperature difference between room and discharge air in the last 10 readings.

5. Show me how many days in November both heating and cooling valves were active simultaneously.

6. What are the min, max, and average values for all air temperature sensors?

7. Show me readings where the fan was running but both valves were closed.

8. Find when room temperature was in the deadband between heating and cooling setpoints.

9. Compare cooling water flow rate with valve position for the last 20 readings.

10. What is the average outdoor air humidity when the damper position is above 50% on October 15th?

11. Find the maximum discharge air temperature on January 10th between 2pm and 6pm.

12. Show me the correlation between fan speed and fan power consumption for the last 50 readings.

13. Calculate the average temperature difference between entering and return water for the heating coil on December 5th.

14. Find periods on November 20th when mixed air humidity exceeded outdoor air humidity.

15. What is the minimum room temperature when the heating valve was fully closed in the last 100 readings?

16. Show me all readings on March 15th from 9am to 5pm where discharge air flow exceeded 500 CFM.

17. Calculate the average cooling coil entering water temperature when the cooling valve is above 75% on July 25th.

18. Find when the return air temperature was higher than the mixed air temperature on September 10th.

19. What is the total fan power consumption between 6am and 8pm on August 12th?

20. Show me readings where outdoor air temperature was below 32°F but the FCU was still operating on February 1st.

21. Calculate the average damper position when outdoor air flow rate is non-zero in the last 30 readings.

22. Find the maximum temperature difference between cooling coil entering and return water on June 5th at 3pm.

23. Show me periods on December 20th when the FCU control mode changed from operate to setback.

24. What is the average room temperature deviation from the heating setpoint on cold days in January?

25. Find readings between 1pm and 4pm on May 18th where all four humidity sensors show values above 60%.

26. Calculate the ratio of outdoor air flow to discharge air flow when the damper is fully open on April 8th.

27. Show me the minimum and maximum fan speed on November 5th when the FCU was in operate mode.

28. Find periods on October 30th from 8am to noon when heating valve position exceeded cooling valve position.

29. What is the average discharge air temperature when both the fan is running and cooling valve is active on July 4th?

30. Show me readings where room temperature setpoint violations occurred for more than 30 minutes on December 15th.

31. Calculate the energy efficiency ratio (cooling delivered vs fan power) for the last 20 readings.

32. Find when the mixed air damper command signal differed from the actual damper position on September 22nd.

33. What is the average heating coil water flow rate when outdoor temperature is below 40°F in December?

34. Show me all readings on March 8th between 10am and 2pm where return air humidity was lowest among all humidity sensors.

35. Calculate the simultaneous operation time of heating and cooling systems on February 28th.

36. Find the peak outdoor air temperature and corresponding indoor conditions on August 20th between 12pm and 6pm.

37. What is the average valve response time (difference between command and position) in the last 50 readings?

38. Show me periods on November 12th when the FCU was operating but fan speed was zero.

39. Calculate the average temperature stratification (difference between discharge and return air) on January 25th from 6pm to 10pm.

40. Find readings where cooling was active despite room temperature being below the cooling setpoint on June 10th.
